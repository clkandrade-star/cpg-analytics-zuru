## What ZURU Is

ZURU Group is a consumer goods, toys, and construction company founded in 2003. It has grown to 5,000+ team members across 30+ global locations and markets worldwide. ZURU describes itself as "one of the fastest-growing and most disruptive companies in the world," built on cutting-edge software, world-leading automation, and advanced manufacturing systems.
