Early Careers at ZURU

## We’re looking for innovative and driven people to Create our Future

Joining ZURU is an opportunity to accelerate your career in a fast-paced environment unlike any other. We’re looking for individuals who radiate positivity and drive, demonstrate humility, have big ideas, who want to help us reimagine tomorrow. Join us in creating our future in a number of different areas. You’ll gain invaluable experience and a deep understanding of what it takes to make it in the competitive world of FMCG & Toys, all while working on exciting global projects that truly make an impact.

Nick Mowbray, Co-Founder
