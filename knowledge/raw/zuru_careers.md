## Joining us

We know that recruitment is a two way street; it’s as much about you making sure ZURU is the right next step, as it is about us making sure you’re the right person to join our team. From the initial call to our ZURU Interview Loop, we have a thorough and transparent process where you can showcase your skills, learn more about the role and the team, and address any questions you may have. We want you to feel confident and excited about joining ZURU, knowing that it aligns with your aspirations and goals.

Please note: This is our general recruitment process, however it may vary for individual roles.

- Click on each title below for more details

Initial Online Call

In this initial call, a member of our Talent Team will catch up with you to discuss your experience, motivation, and goals. We’ll also take this opportunity to provide you with more information about the specific role and give you insights into ZURU’s work and culture.

First Interview

This interview will likely be conducted with your potential leader, who will delve deeper into your experience and expertise.

This is a great chance for you to gain a better understanding of the day-to-day responsibilities associated with the role and the team at ZURU.

Task & Presentation

We’ll provide you with a job-related task to complete and present to the team (don’t worry you’ll have lots of time to complete the task!). This task is specifically designed to give you an understanding of the type of work you would be involved in and give you an opportunity to showcase your technical abilities. It’s also a fantastic opportunity for you to demonstrate your unique approach and problem-solving skills.

ZURU Interview Loop

During this final interview stage, you will have the opportunity to meet various team members with whom you will be working with. These interviews focus on assessing your alignment with our ZURU DNA (see below) through behavioural style questions. We recommend reflecting on your previous experiences as you prepare for this interview. Furthermore, it’s an excellent opportunity for you to ask any questions you may have for the team.

Employment Offer

Following a decision-making meeting and reference checks, your future leader will personally reach out to you with an offer to join our team at ZURU. Congratulations!

## Our DNA

- Click on each title below for more details

Good Humans Only

1. Our team members are either ‘creating our future’ or ‘have lived in our future’
2. Talent density
3. Experts leading experts
4. Specialisation/hyperspecialisation — only the best become generalists
5. Meritocracy
6. Everyone is responsible for HR

Collaboration

1. Team members rather than employees
2. We, not I
3. Build strong frameworks, let the magic happen
4. Leadership is on the dance floor not the balcony

Radical Candour

1. Constructively dissatisfied
2. Radical feedback
3. Brains trust — constantly judging our own work
4. Relentlessly looking for actionable insights
5. Debate hard. Stress test ideas to get the best outcome

Overprepare, and Win

1. Look after the micro and the macro will look after itself
2. Work out how to win the battle before entering the arena
3. Simplification. Rationalise. Streamline
4. Always be thinking about your ROT and ROI
5. Make 10 decisions fast to get 7 right, rather than 2 slow decisions to get both right
6. The Four Pillars of Persuasion
7. Be obsessed with the details
8. Develop pipelines

Shift the Needle

01. Fire bullets. Fail fast
02. Speed of innovation
03. Always be experimenting
04. Getting-things-done mentality
05. Less fluff, more doing
06. Never stop hustling
07. Persistence, persistence, persistence
08. Work product and marketing assets hard
09. CTPs – Creation Touch Points
10. Fight the creep

Compounding Improvement

1. Aim for 2% improvement per week (improvement compounds)
2. Set high standards even for small things
3. Complacency is our enemy
4. Relentless pursuit of marginal gains
5. We suck now compared to where we will be in the future
6. Cross-pollinate and share learnings always
