## Nous recherchons des personnes innovantes et motivées pour créer notre avenir.

Rejoindre ZURU, c'est l'occasion d'accélérer votre carrière dans un environnement dynamique sans pareil. Nous recherchons des personnes qui rayonnent de positivité et de motivation, font preuve d'humilité, ont de grandes idées et souhaitent nous aider à réinventer l'avenir. Rejoignez-nous pour créer notre avenir dans différents domaines. Vous acquerrez une expérience inestimable et une compréhension approfondie de ce qu'il faut pour réussir dans le monde concurrentiel des produits de grande consommation et des jouets, tout en travaillant sur des projets internationaux passionnants qui ont un réel impact.

Nick Mowbray, cofondateur

Our Edge Commercial teams help to drive growth in revenue and profitability globally for our FMCG business. As an intern, you can expect to be involved across a range of markets and retailers, in business development, commercial modelling, strategic planning and much more, with exposure to multiple teams across countries and across the business!

Brand Marketing at ZURU is focused on building strong consumer connections through compelling storytelling, strategic campaigns, and data-driven insights to drive global brand engagement. As an intern, you’ll work with a high-energy team to localize content, support influencer collaborations, conduct market research, and help execute best-in-class brand strategies worldwide.

Content Marketing at ZURU is about creating engaging content that builds brand awareness and connects with consumers across social platforms. As an intern, you'll work with dynamic teams to develop organic content, film and edit short-form videos, monitor trends, and help grow our digital presence globally.

The Paid Media team at ZURU delivers some of the most innovative campaigns in the world, operating at the forefront of channels like TikTok, YouTube, and Snapchat. Interns are an integral part of our team, creating campaigns from scratch on those platforms and analyzing their performance.

As an intern with our Data and Analytics Team, you can expect to be involved in collecting, managing and storing data across the globe on our cloud infrastructure with sources from marketing to sales to logistics. Then make that data useful by enriching it with advanced analytical applications, statistical analysis and machine learning to deliver accessible and actionable insights to key decision makers through the business.

Our Talent Acquisition team works to find and bring top talent into ZURU, working across a range of our international locations. As an intern in this team you can expect to be involved in end-to-end recruitment, including sourcing, screening and interviewing candidates and working with leaders in the business.

Our Logistics team optimize global distribution using data-driven forecasting and advanced modeling while managing millions in inventory. We collaborate with global teams across New Product Development, Sales, and Customer Service in a dynamic, tech-forward environment.

Our Toys Commercial teams are responsible for driving sales and brand strategy of ZURU Toys across global markets. As an intern in this team, you'll be exposed to a variety of business areas, including sales, New Product Development and licensing. You’ll also gain hands-on experience in a range of work including identifying market gaps and opportunities and building sales tools like brand decks and case studies to drive commercial success.

Our Brand Engagement team amplifies ZURU’s social media presence by delivering high-impact, creator-led content through influencer partnerships, video editing, and performance-driven storytelling across global channels.

Licensing at ZURU focuses on building and managing partnerships with global brands and partners, working cross-functionally with Legal, Commercial, and Product Development teams to bring licensed products to life.

The AI Integration team accelerates AI adoption across ZURU by embedding within business units to identify opportunities, implement practical solutions, and build AI fluency. As an intern, you’ll work closely with internal teams to deliver workshops, launch quick-win AI initiatives, and help integrate AI tools into daily workflows to boost productivity and innovation.

The Ecommerce Content team partners with Brand and Marketing to create high-performing product content that drives discoverability and conversion across retail platforms. As an intern, you’ll help optimize and test ecommerce content for AI-powered search environments, support AEO and LLM-focused initiatives, and collaborate cross-functionally to build consistent, data-driven, and AI-ready content across ZURU brands.
